The Langenium Universe


31st January 2015

The entire story is set in the Langenium Universe. All historical events from the real world are retained however the story projects itself far into the past and future.


As this is an open source project, all creative development including notes, artwork and storywriting are available on Github. The only proprietary tool currently used is Adobe Illustrator and unfortunately it is required to open a lot of the artwork


The Langenium timeline below shows which games are currently planned as well as which major arcs make up the story. The different coloured strips are dimensional realms that make up a single universe.
