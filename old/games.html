Epoch Exordium


Release date: 2015~2016


Epoch Exordium is a single player RPG currently in development


The story is about an astronaut, Jack. Jack wakes up on the Helios Station with memory troubles. The onboard AI WERP guides him through operating and repairing the systems on the station in order to produce exordium and send it to Earth.


Music has been sourced from various classical composers. Soundtrack available on Soundcloud.
